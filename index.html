Quick Hair Care Guide

Follow these and your hair will be as healty as a baby's

  • Avoid hot showers and shampoo your scalp, not your ends
  • Wash your hair two to three times a week, max
  • Towel-dry your hair before applying conditioner
  • Brush your hair twice a day, from the bottom up
  • Use a low-heat setting for hairdrying and point the nozzle down, not side-on
  • Try castor oil, hair oil and dry shampoo
